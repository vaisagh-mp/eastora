Tours

At Eastora Holidays, we believe that travel is more than just visiting places it's about creating lasting memories, experiencing cultures, and discovering yourself along the way. Creating unforgettable travel experiences across India and beyond.

Headquartered in Kochi, Kerala, we are a passionate tour and destination management company offering customized travel solutions across India and the globe. Whether you're a solo traveller, a family, a group of friends, or a corporate client, our mission is to craft journeys that are seamless, enriching, and deeply personal.

From the golden dunes of Rajasthan to the pristine beaches of Bali, from spiritual sojourns in Varanasi to luxury escapes in the Maldives every itinerary we design is tailored with care, backed by local expertise, and delivered with a commitment to excellence..

Our goal is to provide individualized vacation experiences that fit your interests and budget while guaranteeing a smooth transition from preparation to memories.

Domestic and International Tours

Discover the diverse charm of India, from the serene backwaters of Kerala to the majestic Himalayan landscapes. We also offer curated travel experiences to global destinations including Dubai, Maldives, Malaysia, Thailand, Nepal, Turkey, and Europe.

Hotels, Flights & Transport

Choose from a wide range of options, from budget-friendly stays to luxurious resorts, along with convenient flight bookings and ground transportation.

Personalised Travel Solutions

Our dedicated team is here to simplify your journey with tailor-made travel plans designed to suit your preferences and to assist you at any point of time of your travel period.

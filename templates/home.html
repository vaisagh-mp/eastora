Faq

What to Know Before
You Go

What types of tour packages does Eastora Holidays offer?
+

We offer a wide range of curated tour packages including domestic tours across India, international holiday packages, customized honeymoon getaways, Ayurveda and wellness retreats, adventure trips, and cultural experiences — all designed to suit your travel style and budget.

Can I customize my travel itinerary with Eastora Holidays?
+

Absolutely! We specialize in personalized travel planning. Whether you want to adjust your itinerary, upgrade accommodation, or include specific experiences, our travel consultants will tailor the package to match your preferences.

Do your packages include flights and visas?
+

Yes, many of our international packages are inclusive of flights and visa assistance. For transparency, each package will clearly mention what's included. We also provide standalone flight bookings and complete visa support as optional add-ons.

What is the booking and payment process?
+

Booking with Eastora Holidays is easy and secure. Once you select a package, our team will guide you through the confirmation process. A booking amount is required to reserve your trip, with the remaining balance payable before departure. Multiple payment options are available for your convenience.

Is travel insurance included in the tour packages?
+

Travel insurance is not included by default but can be added to your package upon request. We highly recommend opting for travel insurance to ensure protection against unforeseen events such as medical emergencies, trip cancellations, or lost baggage.

How can I get in touch with Eastora Holidays for more information or assistance?
+

You can reach us through our website's contact form, email us at info@eastoraholidays.com , or call our customer support number. Our travel advisors are available to assist you with any queries, bookings, or custom travel requirements.
